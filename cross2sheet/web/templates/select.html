This program takes an image of a crossword grid and converts it into a spreadsheet that can be uploaded to Google Sheets.

Image files:

Right click on the crossword grid, select, "Save Image As", and then upload the image here.

PDF files:

Download the PDF, and then upload it here.

Otherwise:

Take a screenshot. Cropping the screenshot might help but usually isn't necessary.
