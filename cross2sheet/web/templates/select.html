This program takes an image of a crossword grid and converts it into a spreadsheet that can be uploaded to Google Sheets. Right click on the crossword grid, select, "Save Image As", and then upload the image here.

Q: What if the grid isn't an image file?

Take a screenshot. I recommend cropping the screenshot to include just the crossword, but the program can sometimes handle screenshots that are not cropped. If the crossword is a PDF file, you can also try uploading that.
